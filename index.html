The problem

Every shipment has too many places to break.

For many Indian businesses, trade work still moves through paperwork, WhatsApp threads, phone calls, broker updates and disconnected spreadsheets. The result is slower decisions, unclear costs and weak shipment visibility.

BL / DOC

Manual documents

Invoices, packing lists, compliance checks and export paperwork are hard to track when every file sits in a different place.

FRT / RATE

Opaque freight

FCL and LCL options are difficult to compare when prices, timelines and carrier details arrive through different channels.

MID / CALL

Too many middlemen

Export assistance, buyers, suppliers, transporters and finance partners often work as separate conversations.

ETA / LOST

No single status

Once goods move, teams still chase updates instead of seeing one live record from booking to delivery.

The platform

One operating flow from registration to delivery.

The early MVP should compress the trade journey into a record that moves with the user: business details, opportunities, documents, logistics decisions and shipment status.

STEP 01

Register and verify

Create the business profile, capture required documents and prepare the account for trade, export or logistics workflows.

STEP 02

Discover the right route

Find buyers, suppliers, logistics partners or trade support based on product, location, timeline and service need.

STEP 03

Document and coordinate

Move compliance, export paperwork, FCL/LCL planning and partner coordination into one digital shipment workspace.

STEP 04

Track and analyze

Follow the movement to delivery with live status, ETA reporting, finance/insurance support and business intelligence.

MVP

PHINSOR is not claiming full market scale yet. The page is inviting pilots and partners to shape which workflows should be built first, tested first and improved with real trade data.

Six operating pillars

A platform stack shaped like the shipment itself.

The services are not isolated feature boxes. They are the working layers a business needs while moving goods from opportunity to booking to clearance to delivery.

Container stack logic

Each layer supports the next decision.

Trade discovery helps identify demand. Documentation makes the movement possible. Booking and tracking make it operational. Analytics and automation make it repeatable.

Who it is built for

Different users, different pressure points.

A farmer group, an MSME exporter and a logistics manager do not need the same story. PHINSOR should meet each one at the point where trade work becomes slow, uncertain or expensive.

MSME track

MSMEs

Find trade support, compare logistics options and reduce the paperwork burden before the shipment becomes too complex.

Exporter track

Exporters and importers

Keep compliance, booking, partner coordination and status updates attached to one shipment workspace.

Agriculture track

Farmers and producer groups

Explore buyer connections, agri-export support and shipment planning without depending only on informal networks.

Industry track

Manufacturers

Plan outward movement, documentation and partner support around production schedules and dispatch requirements.

Logistics track

Logistics companies

Participate as service partners, provide reliable movement options and build visibility into customer workflows.

Enterprise track

Enterprise customers

Evaluate the platform as a structured partner for digital trade workflows, reporting and industrial technology adoption.
